The Best Movies According to Belema

My Top 3 Movies All Time

Inception

This is the greatest psychological thriller ever and one of Leonardo Dicaprio's greatest acting performances.

The Notebook

Greatest love story ever that makes you believe in true and everlasting love. Ending is amazing.

Remember the Titans

One of my favorite Denzel movies. An ode to all sports fans and an representation of the fight for social justice and civil rights and oppurtunities for colored people.
